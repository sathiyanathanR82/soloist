Data Deletion Request

We value your privacy and your right to be forgotten.

⚠️ Important Information

Please note that once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. This includes your profile information, photos, and all associated account data.

How to delete your data:

  1. Option 1: In-App (Recommended)
    • Log in to your account.
    • Go to your Profile.
    • Select Settings and find the Delete Account option.
  2. Option 2: Contact Support
    • Email us at support@soloist.com from your registered email address.
    • Use the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
    • Provide your full name and account email for verification.

Need help? Reach out to us at privacy@soloist.com
